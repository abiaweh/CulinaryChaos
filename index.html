So... What Is Culinary Chaos?

Are you new to cooking? Burnt food still haunts you? You're in the right place! This is your safe, no-judgement zone for learning how to make real food - the kind you actually want to eat. From random cravings to comfort meals (with a halal-friendly twist), we'll guide you from the basics to higher levels. Expect a little mess, some laughs and recipes that might just make you the designated cook.

Let's get started by not burning the house down - and turning those burnt attempts into delicious wins!
